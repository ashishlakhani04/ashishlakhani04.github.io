AlgoBid

Don't bid on bitcoins when you can bid on Algorithms

Welcome to Code-Auction, a unique competition which stitches the thrill of bidding and the challenge of coding into a seamless fabric. Where the questions for which you win the bid, has to be solved by your teammate. Code auction gives you an opportunity to be a part of a bidding contest where you can bid our money on your friend's knowledge. Details for Auction round: All the teams will be given a certain amount of credit for bidding. Both the teammates will auction questions for each other. (i. e. Questions won by 'A' in bidding will have to be solved by 'B' and vice versa) Questions will be segregated on the basis of difficulty level: Easy, Medium and Hard. Each question will have a certain amount of points associated with it depending upon the difficulty level. The teams will bid their money against other teams to buy the questions. The other member of the team will be presented with the questions which their fellow teammates bought, if answered correctly, points will be rewarded according to the level of questions. The team with the highest number of points in their accounts will be the ultimate champion of Code auction! This competition consists of 2 rounds :
Round 1 :- Auction
Round 2 :- Coding
