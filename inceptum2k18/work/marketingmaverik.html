ABOUT TRIVIA FAQ's

“Bring the best of your authentic self to every opportunity.”

A game to judge marketing skills, bargaining power, ability to convince customers and team work.

Problem Statement :

  • To earn maximum profit with the capital allocated.

Rounds and details :

  • It is a team event of one round only (Time Duration 2 hours apx)
  • Each team should have 3-4 members. Contestants will be provided with the fix amount of fund (as initial capital).
  • Team will have to take a selfie with the customer along with amount collected. There must be a volunteer with each team and all the transactions must be collected by volunteer.
  • Participants can not add any amount in the capital provided to them. Winners will be awarded with cash prize & certificates.
  • All participants must carry their college ID cards.

Judging Criteria :

  • Team will be judged on the basis of maximum amount collected by investing the given amount
  • In case of two team earn same amount of profit, then the one who came first will be the winner.
