To motivate students towards full development of their potentials in enhancing their knowledge while having fun in learning.

Rounds and details ::

  • It is a team event. Each team should have 2-3 members.
  • There will be three rounds. Each round is eliminating round.
  • Stationery will be provided by coordinators.
  • Judges decisions will be final and binding.
  • Winners will be awarded with cash prize and certificates.
  • Every team must carry their college ID Card.
  • Number of rounds :- 3
    1st round :- Aptitude test (50 ques )
    2nd round :- 3 sub rounds (random questions, video, images)
    3rd round :- logo logic and tag line

Rules :

  • First round will be an aptitude test of 50 questions and time limit will be of 20 minutes.
  • Second round will consist of 3 sub rounds of random questions, questions based on an image and video.
  • Third round will be logo logic and tag line based.
  • All rounds are elimination rounds.
  • Team composition cannot be changed once the prelim has been taken
  • 15 seconds time will be given to the team for answering their respective question in round 2 and 3

Judging Criteria ::

  • If any team will not be able to give correct answer to any ques then that ques will automatically will be passed to next team..
  • The passed ques will carry half mark for next team.
  • In case of any dispute, the decision of judges will be final.
