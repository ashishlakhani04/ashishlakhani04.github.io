“Be the Best Version of You”

The event prepares the participants for the recruitment process. The participants get grilled with questions and different selection rounds procedure that they would normally undergo in a recruitment process. It’s a great experience before the actual process. (A game to judge your professional skills, job skills, body language and the art of conversation with others.)

Problem Statement :

  • You have to express yourself in limited atmosphere and also your knowledge at the right time in limited Timing.

Rounds and details :

    There will be three rounds in Carpe Diem.

  • ROUND I:
    In the virtual round, each team has to describe the idea/mechanism to the judges. A team copying the mechanism from the internet or failing to describe or justify its mechanism would stand disqualified. The cost of Manufacturing will also be evaluated. The cheaper, the better.
  • The dress code for both boys and girls, for this round will be formals.
  • Questions will be in English Language.
  • Selection will be based on obtaining Marks in Written Test.
  • Decision of the judges would be considered final and binding in any situation.
  • Question will be consisting of General Management, Reasoning, Maths, Economics, General Knowledge related to Business Sector.

    • Instruction for written paper:
  • There will be 50 questions in the written test. Time duration for the written test will be 20 minutes.
  • Every Question have four options. You have to choose right answer and write down on your answer sheet.
  • There will not any negative marking for the questions.All questions are compulsory and carry equal marks.
  • Use only black and blue ball pen to fill the appropriate answer in answer sheet. To bring and use logbook, calculator, pager and cellular phone in event room is prohibited.

    • ROUND II
  • This round will be as Group Discussion. The dress code for both boys and girls, for this round will be formals.
  • Time duration will be 8 min to 10 min for Every Batch.There will no compulsion for language.
  • The Batches will be consisting of 4 to 6 Students. It is advised everyone to treat one another with respect and there should be no name calling and personal attacks.
  • Students of this round will go to next and final round. Topics for Group Discussion will be as Follows:
    Economic Topics
    Current Topics
    Management Topics
    Current Issues
    Political Topics related to Govt Business Policy
  • ROUND III:
  • This Round Will be as Personal Interview/Stress Interview. The dress code for both boys and girls, for this round will be formals.
  • There will no compulsion for language.
  • Time Duration for Each Student will be 10-15 Min.
  • Selection will be based on the performance (PROFFESIONAL SKILLS, STRENGTHS, ART OF RESPONDING etc) in Interview.
  • “Please try to reach on time. If you get late, You will be disqualified from the any round of the Placement Drive”

    • Judging Criteria ::

    • There will be three judges.
    • PJudgement will be based on your performance, confidence level infront of Panel of Judges and marks obtained by you.
    • In case of two candidates get same marks in the Final result, then prize will be distributed between both candidates.
