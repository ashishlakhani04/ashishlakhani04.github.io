DRIVERS DETAILS:

  • Age- Every team is supposed to have two drivers and both the drivers must be at least 18 years of age.
  • Driver’s License- Each driver must have a valid Driver’s License (Four Wheeler) issued by the Government of India (Learner’s license is also allowed). Both drivers must carry the driving license in original and must be able to produce at the time of the event.
  • Medical Insurance of the drivers- Both the drivers must have a valid medical insurance with full accidental cover, which must be presented on the event site.
  • Drivers Safety Gear- The following are the minimum requirements and restrictions that will be enforced through technical inspection, at any stage of competition. Noncompliance if any observed by the inspection/organizing/judging committee members must be corrected and no vehicles without passing the technical inspection would be allowed to participate further in the event. All the parts of Driver’s Safety Gear must meet the required rating (specified). No driver would be allowed to drive the vehicle without the complete driver’s safety gear in any of the dynamic event. The complete driver’s safety gear of KARTWARS will consist of the following items: Driver’s Suit,Helmet, Gloves, Shoes, mouth guard
  • Driver Agreement- the driver and the captain would be required to sign an agreement at the site of the event regarding the safety of the driver and the kart.
  • In case of any accidental damage to the kart or injury to the driver, the committee will not be responsible for the loss of the particular team.

KART'S REQUIREMENTS:

    • Chassis Design Requirements- Light weight chassis are preferred, however this does not permit the teams to produce any kind of holes in the chassis. The vehicle must have a wheelbase of at least 1143mm (45 inch). The wheelbase is measured from the center of contact on ground of the front to rear tires with the wheels pointed straight ahead.
    • Wheelbase and Track Width- The wheelbase of the vehicle must be between 40-55 inch and the smaller track width (front or rear) must be no less than 80% of the wheelbase of the vehicle.
    • Ground Clearance- under all conditions, the minimum ground clearance of the kart should be 1”.
    • Braking System- The kart must be equipped with a braking system which is capable of stopping the kart within a distance of 100ft when the kart is moving at a speed of 35km/hr. Electronic braking systems are strictly prohibited.
    • Wheels and Tyres- Teams are free to use any of the tyres compatible to their setup meeting the necessary requirements of the rules and regulations.

PRECAUTIONS:

  • All team members are required to cooperate with, and follow all instructions from competition organizers, officials and judges.
  • No teams would be allowed to drive the kart inside the campus until permitted to do so. In case of non compliance of the rule, the decision of the committee will be final.
  • All the technical queries will be answered through proper mailing channel or telephone. None of the queries regarding the event will be entertained after 2 days from the end of the event

BEHAVIOUR:

    • In case of misconduct by any team member, the respective team will be warned. In case of any further misbehavior, a penalty of 50 points shall be imposed.
    • Argument with, or disobedience to, any official may result in the team being eliminated from the competition.

VEHICLE RELATED:

    • Off track vehicles must be pushed at a normal walking pace by means of Push Bar/members pushing the vehicle, with all four (4) wheels on the ground, a team member sitting in the cockpit to steer and brake and with another team member walking beside the car. During performance events when the excitement is high, it is particularly important that the car be moved at a slow pace in the pits. Violation of this rule will result directly into the points penalty.

FACE-OFF:

    • This is a 1 on 1 racing event. For this, a 2 lane track would be setup with flags at equal intervals on each side alternatively. The team captains would be required to choose the tracks based on a toss. In order to win the event, the driver will have to cross the finish line first after collecting all the flags. In case of odd number of teams, a team would be given a bye and promoted to next round based on a lottery and points would be awarded accordingly. The winners of each round would be promoted to next round to compete with each other. The number of rounds will be based on the number of participants. For example if there are 20 teams, 10 groups would be competing in the first round. The 10 winners would be divided in 5 groups to compete against each other. Now amongst the 5 winners, one of the teams would be given a bye and remaining 2 groups would compete among themselves. Out of the 3 teams progressing to this round (1 bye and 2 winners), one would again be given a bye and promoted to the next round i.e. the final round and the remaining two teams would compete in order to face the team already promoted to the final round. In the final round, a winner will be decided.

PODIUM FINISH:

    • In this event teams would be competing with each other in a track race. The teams will be divided into groups, each group having 2 teams. These groups will then compete among themselves and the winners of each group shall be promoted to next round. These winners would then be competing against each other in the same fashion. The committee shall decide whether the teams will be given a bye or compete with other teams to be promoted to the next round in case they fail to form a group. In case of any accident, a whistle or siren would be blown by the volunteers present by the side of the track and the teams whether or not involved in the accident would be required to stop their kart on the spot.

SKID PAD:

    • For this event, a track shown below would be setup with a minimum of radius 2.5m and track width of 2.5m. The team covering the track in shortest time shall win the event. The team would penalized in case of the touching or crossing cones that would surround the track. Each team would be allowed a maximum of 3 attempts. In case of more than one attempts, the timing of the latest attempt would be considered.
